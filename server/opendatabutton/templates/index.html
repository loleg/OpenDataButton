About

This OKCon Law Mining Hackathon project aims to illuminate legal and technical restrictions on data, and create grounds for simple community action for changes to terms of use. The Open This Data! button is an easy, social way to share the fact that you are prevented from accessing data in some way.

To get started, drag this button to your bookmark bar:

Open This Data!

Goals

  1. Develop crowdsourcing of not-so-open data
  2. Make an easy way of sharing restrictions
  3. Visualize the “not-so-open-data map”
